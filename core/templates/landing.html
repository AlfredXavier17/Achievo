What Are SMART Goals?

SMART stands for Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound. For example, instead of 'Exercise,' set 'Run 5km in 30 days.' Studies (e.g., from the American Psychological Association) show that goal-setting with reflection boosts success by up to 25%.
